Verse — lyrics in your notch.

A macOS menu bar app that shows time-synced lyrics for whatever's playing, anchored right to your MacBook's notch. Invisible when idle, tiny when playing, beautiful when you want it.

Download for Mac
macOS 14+ · Apple Silicon · free
Release notes
Ad-hoc signed — on first launch, right-click the app and choose "Open".
Invisible when idle Nothing playing, nothing shown. Your notch stays exactly as Apple made it.
Tiny when playing A sliver of album art and the current line, sized to never collide with your menu bar.
Beautiful when you want it Hover to expand into a full karaoke panel tinted by the album art.

Features

Time-synced lyrics

Pulled from LRCLIB's free, open catalog and matched to the exact track, album, and duration you're hearing.

Hover for vibe mode

A karaoke panel grows out of the notch, tinted with the dominant color from the album art.

Click any line to seek

Jump playback straight to a lyric — instant, no scrubbing required.

Four animation themes

From expressive type-on and word spotlight to a minimal underline tracer — pick your energy.

Spotify & Apple Music

Works with whichever one you already use, no extra setup.

Cached offline

Lyrics are saved locally after the first fetch, so repeat plays work without a connection.
